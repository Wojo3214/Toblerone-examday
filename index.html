Our part of history

Toblerone was founded in 1908 by the Swiss chocolate producer Theodor Tobler and his Cousin Emil Baumann, who came up with the idea of mixing chocolate with almond nougat and honey after trying the nougat while traveling to Italy. Instead of creating a traditional flat chocolate bar, Tobler designed a form in the shape of a triangular prism from which small portions could be break and eaten.
