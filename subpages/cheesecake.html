Cheesecake

Source: yummly.com

  • 1 cup plain chocolate biscuits (chocolate wafer cookies)
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 packages (8 ounce size) cream cheese, softened, at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup thickened cream, or heavy cream
  • 8 ounces Toblerone chocolate (milk or dark chocolate)

Firstly, you have to melt the butter, then crush chocolate biscquits. Next, mix everything together biscuits crumbs and melted butter. After that put this to some form and put to the fridge.

Okay, now when you're waiting for the mixture you can do next step. Beat the cream cheese until smooth. Next, melt the Toblerone and add sugar and cream cheese. Still beating until combined

Now you can pour the mixture into base and put to the fridge for 2-3 hours.

Enjoy your meal!
